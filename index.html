Ideal Job

The role of IT architect is to evaluate system demands, then uses professional skills to design and maintain computer network for organizations and business companies. Their duties include improving, maintaining and modifying current network systems. IT architects may act as manager and are responsible for solving IT related problems and issues. In the management roles, IT architects also have the opportunity to in- charge of the IT department in company and are responsible for scheduling and assessing budgetary necessity which may include estimating upgrade costs or switching Internet services. This kind of job like working for a business especially in the management role which relates to IT is my dream job. This is because besides having an interest in IT, I in person like business so much. Since IT architect’s jobs involves in both business and IT field, so I think this jobs suits me well because I can work in workplace where I can find the things that I like and have interests in. In order to become a IT architects, qualified skills are required. A typical Bachelor’s degree in information systems management or related fields is needed to get an employment. A Mater’s degree would give a better advantage. An entry-level position as a systems architect can provide the necessary experience for a higher-level position. Entry-level job duties for IT architect might include planning and supervising projects, solving issues, and participating in meetings. Higher-level job duties include providing development support and assistance, writing processes, recording results, and managing projects. As an IT architect, 4 main skills are needed. There are IT, design ,sector and consulting knowledge. The skills that I will have after graduated from my Bachelor’s degree would be just enough for me to start my career. New graduates usually begin with introductory classes, such as knowledge organization, database creation and operating system basics, those who have advanced in the course may go through more deeply into intermediate topics including management support systems, network security and system design. For the senior position, usually a minimum of 5 years of experience are required. The plan that I currently have for me to become a IT architect is to get a bachelor degree in Computer Science. This is the lowest requirement. After that, participating in internship. Internship program that are offered provide hands-on experience for fresh graduates. The experience gained can be invaluable when looking for employment. A master degree would also give a more advantage when looking for job that required more knowledge and skills.
